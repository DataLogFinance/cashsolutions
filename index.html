CashSolutions Treasury Software listing

CashSolutions is made of:

  • CashRisk (both financial operation software and risk management system)
  • CashValue (Cash Management software)
  • CashPooler (Payment and Collection Platform + eBAM management + Bank Mobility management + SEPA)

DataLog Finance has besides developped the first full Web smart TMS (Treasury Management System) Treasury Line, with an innovative approach : Front to Back / to Book / to Payment. The treasurers can thus have in a single software and a single database : trade and market transactions management, risk management, cash management, payment management, bank communication, entry accounting... In a comprehensive functional environment (multi-instruments, multi-markets, multi-protocols, multi-formats, unlimited transactions...)
